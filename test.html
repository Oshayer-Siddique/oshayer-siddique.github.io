Throughout my career, I have maintained a dual focus on rigorous academic research and practical business application. My approach combines systematic investigation with entrepreneurial vision, enabling me to identify opportunities that others might overlook and develop solutions that address real-world challenges.

As a researcher, I have contributed to various fields through peer-reviewed publications, conference presentations, and collaborative projects. My research methodology emphasizes empirical evidence, statistical analysis, and reproducible results. I believe that quality research must be both methodologically sound and practically relevant.

In my entrepreneurial capacity, I have founded and advised multiple ventures, focusing on technology-driven solutions that create sustainable value. My business philosophy centers on evidence-based decision making, strategic planning, and long-term growth rather than short-term gains.

I am particularly interested in the intersection of research and business, where academic insights can be transformed into market solutions. This perspective has allowed me to contribute meaningfully to both academic discourse and commercial innovation.

Selected Publications

"Digital Transformation in South Asian SMEs: A Longitudinal Study"
Journal of Business Research, Vol. 45, No. 3, 2023
This comprehensive study examines the digital transformation journey of 150 small and medium enterprises across South Asia over a five-year period, identifying key success factors and common barriers to digital adoption.
"Entrepreneurial Networks and Innovation Capacity in Emerging Markets"
International Entrepreneurship Review, Vol. 28, No. 2, 2022
An empirical analysis of how entrepreneurial networks contribute to innovation capacity in emerging markets, based on data from 12 countries and 300 startups.
"Sustainable Venture Capital: Impact Measurement and Long-term Value Creation"
Academy of Management Perspectives, Vol. 36, No. 1, 2022
A framework for evaluating the long-term impact of venture capital investments that prioritize environmental and social outcomes alongside financial returns.
"Technology Transfer Mechanisms in University-Industry Partnerships"
Research Policy, Vol. 51, No. 4, 2021
Analysis of 25 successful university-industry partnerships to identify effective mechanisms for technology transfer and commercialization of academic research.
